5. Our team

Your challenge is here.

In teams, describe your mascot.

Then, individually, describe yourselves.

And finally, present to your classmates!

1. Your final task

1. Create your mascot

Use a piece of paper. You have examples here.

2. Design your avatar

You can do it online or using a piece of paper. You have examples here.

3. Write your mascot's description

Do this activity with your team. You have the structure here.

4. Write your personal description

Do this activity individually. You have the structure here.

5. Prepare your oral presentation

Here you have some tips for the oral presentation.

6. Do the oral presentation

And that's it!

2. Evidence

What evidence does the teacher need?

  • IN TEAMS: Mascot (drawing, text with its description and participation in the oral presentation) and coevaluation.
  • INDIVIDUAL: digital avatar, text with personal description and oral presentation.
