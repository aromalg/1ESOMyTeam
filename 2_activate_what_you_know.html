2. Activate what you know

Before presenting your team or finding your new mascot,

you need to review some vocabulary.

Do you know the parts of the body?

They are important for a physical description!

1. Body parts

a) Watch the following video in teams. 

b) Take turns to write and translate the parts of the body in your notebooks. Write as many words as you know.

Rotating page

Use this technique to write your words in English.

Time to practise

I am sure you know many of these words. Now it is time to practise with some activities. Ask your teacher!
