4.3.Tips to present your team

1. Preparing a good oral presentation

Structure of the presentation

  • Start with an initial greeting:
    • Good morning, classmates!
    • Good afternoon and welcome to our presentation!
  • Introduce the team. Say the names of the participants and their roles:
    • This is the green team and there are four of us... Susan is our secretary.
    • Let us introduce ouselves: This is Peter and he is our manager.
  • Show your team's logo.
    • This is our logo. It's a...
    • As you can see, this is our logo. It's...
  • Take turns to describe your mascot. Show it to your classmates.
    • As you can see, this is our mascot. Its name is...
    • This is ... It's our mascot because...
  • Individually, describe yourselves. Show your avatars.
    • Use your writing but do not read.
  • Finish with a concluding sentence.
    • That's all. Thank you very much.
    • Thanks for listening.

Tips for a good presentation

  • Speak clearly and slowly.
  • Pronounce correctly. Ask if you don't know before presenting.
  • Watch your body language even if it is not your turn.
  • Look at the public.
