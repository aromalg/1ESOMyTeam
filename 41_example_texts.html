4.1. Example texts

1. Reading comprehension

OUR MASCOT MAX

My name is Max. I am from Hairy World and I am 235 years old. I go to the Mascot school and I am in Year 5. I live in a cave with my family.

I am short and very hairy. I have got thick eyebrows, short legs and orange fur. In the picture, I am wearing white and blue trainers and I have got a green and red umbrella.

I am very grumpy, but also adventurous. I like chess and learning new languages, and my favourite subject is P.E.

See you soon!

JOHN WARD

My name is John Ward. I am from Dublin (Ireland)and I am twelve years old. I go to Oxford boarding school and I am in year 7. I live there with my teachers and my classmates. 

I'm tall and slim. I've got dark short hair and my eyes are blue. In the picture, I've got glasses and I'm wearing a blue t-shirt and brown shorts. 

I am very friendly, and sometimes shy.  I like football, videogames and meeting friends, and my favourite subject is maths. 

I'm ready to work hard with my team this year!

3. What you need to know

Remember the paragraphs to write a good description:

Paragraph 1: Introduction

  • Name
  • City and country
  • Age
  • School
  • Grade
  • Residence
  • Lives with...

Paragraph 2: Physical description

  • Hair colour and length
  • Eye colour
  • Accesories
  • Clothes

Paragraph 3: Personality

  • Personality adjectives
  • Hobbies and preferences
  • Favourite subject

Paragraph 4: Conclusion

  • A concluding sentence

A. Order the paragraphs

Order the paragraphs. Remember the structure of a description:

  • This is my friend Marie. She is 14 years old and she lives in Paris, France.
  • She lives with her aunt and two cousins and she is in Third Grade in Molière school.
  • She is blonde. Her hair is straight and her eyes are green. She always wears a hat.
  • My friend Marie is hard-working and nice, and she likes chess.
  • Marie is my best friend! I love her!
